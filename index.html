About the Experiment

$FOMO Coin is not just another cryptocurrency; it's an experiment in social behavior and digital value creation. The idea is simple yet revolutionary: everyone tweets out the contract address (CA) of the coin, creating unparalleled visibility and engagement. As more people join in and share the CA, the experiment becomes a living, breathing entity powered by collective action. The question is, how far can it go?

STAGES

  1. Launch $FOMO coin
  2. Everyone tweets out the CA
  3. Watch as the price goes up

Experiment Outline

Independent Variable: Number of tweets

Dependent Variable: Price of $FOMO

Control Group: Nonexposed Twitter users
